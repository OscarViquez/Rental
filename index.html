There are 3 large bedrooms and 3 full baths, each with full shower/tub combination. There are two master suites, each with fabulous king-size beds and private baths. The third bedroom is so large it has two wonderful full size beds and a private full bath with a sliding glass door to the private patio/deck. There are gorgeous pool and golf course views from the dinning room, family room and master bedroom! There is also a full size washer and dryer for your use.

Our house is just a five minute walk to the first tee of the RBC Heritage Golf Tournament on Harbour Town Golf Links and all the amenities of Harbour Town including The Harbour Town Lighthouse, boutique shopping, the Harbour Town Marina, Gregg Russell HT playground, the HT Pier (kids can go crabbing!), The Quarterdeck, The Crazy Crab Restaurant and CQ's!
